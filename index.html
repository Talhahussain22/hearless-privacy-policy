03

Audio Processing & Backend Infrastructure

When you submit audio for transcription, your audio file is sent securely to our private backend server hosted on Render (render.com). Our backend acts as a secure intermediary between the app and the transcription API.

Security Note All API keys and credentials (including our OpenAI API key) are stored exclusively on our backend server. They are never embedded in or exposed through the mobile application. This architecture ensures your data is handled securely at all times.

Our backend then forwards the audio to OpenAI's Whisper API for speech-to-text transcription. Audio data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Audio files are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently on our servers unless required for specific functionality. Temporary files are automatically purged after processing is complete.

We do not use your audio for advertising, model training, or any purpose unrelated to providing the transcription service you requested.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, including:

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
