Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hearless – Audio Transcriber ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Audio Data

Hearless allows users to upload, record, or share audio files for transcription purposes. These audio files are processed to convert speech into text.

We do not permanently store your audio files unless explicitly required for processing.

1.2 Transcription Data

The text generated from your audio may be temporarily processed to deliver results within the app.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage data, including:

  • App features used
  • Session duration
  • User interactions

1.4 Device Information

We may collect limited device-related information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
---

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide audio-to-text transcription services
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To monitor usage and prevent abuse
  • To maintain and enhance service quality
---

3. Audio Processing and Storage

Audio files are processed only for transcription purposes. We do not use your audio content for training, marketing, or any unrelated purposes.

Audio data may be temporarily transmitted to secure servers or third-party services to perform transcription.

---

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase Analytics: for app usage insights
  • RevenueCat: for managing in-app purchases

  • Cloud transcription services: for audio processing

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share data:

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Audio files are not stored permanently unless required for functionality.
