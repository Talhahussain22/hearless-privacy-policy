Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hearless – Audio Transcriber ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Audio Data

Hearless allows users to upload, record, or share audio files for transcription. This includes audio shared from other applications via the device's share feature (share intent).

Audio files are processed only to convert speech into text.

1.2 Transcription Data

The text generated from your audio is processed and displayed within the app.

1.3 Account Information

When you sign in using our app, we use authentication services (such as Firebase Authentication). This may collect:

  • Email address
  • User ID

1.4 Stored User Data

We may store limited user-related data (such as usage credits or app-related data) to provide and manage app functionality.

1.5 Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage data, including:

  • App interactions
  • Session duration
  • Features used

1.6 Device Information

We may collect limited device-related information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
---

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide audio transcription services
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To manage user credits and purchases
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To prevent misuse and ensure security
---

3. Audio Processing

Audio files (including those shared from other apps) are processed only for transcription purposes. We do not use your audio for advertising or unrelated purposes.

---

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication: for user login and account management
  • Firebase Analytics: for usage insights
  • RevenueCat: for managing in-app purchases
  • Google Play Billing: for secure payments
  • Cloud transcription services: for audio processing

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers required to operate the app
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud or misuse

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services. Audio files are not stored permanently unless required for functionality.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.
